Tokenizaiton step by step

Preparation
Creation
Listing
Asset management

Step 1. Preparation

We work out the legal and practical basis for the token emission, help determine its starting price and develop a technical task for creating the infrastructure.

Step 2. Creation

We develop the infrastructure, issue your token, train your staff in technical work or provide additional services. We conduct a preliminary sale of your token to a closed pool of investors.

Step 3. Listing

We list your tokens on agreed platforms and release them for sale on your and our infrastructure.

Step 4. Asset management

We carry out marketing and sales management of a digital asset, provide technical support or transfer a fully formed and functional product to your team.
